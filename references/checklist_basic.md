# Basic Review Checklist

## Legality
1. Subject qualification and authority chain are valid.
2. Contract purpose and content do not violate mandatory rules.
3. Execution formalities are complete (signature/seal/annexes/date).

## Compliance
1. Regulatory requirements and industry constraints are reflected.
2. Internal制度/审批 requirements are met.
3. Evidence and filing obligations are executable.

## Reasonableness
1. Rights and obligations are balanced.
2. Liability allocation matches control ability.
3. Dispute mechanism is practical and enforceable.
4. The clauses fit the likely next scenario, not only current signing.

## Corporate and Regulatory Specific Checks
1. Check listed-company constraints: board/shareholder approvals, disclosure, related-party process.
2. Check financial/quasi-financial constraints: regulatory confirmation, asset segregation, capital use boundaries.
3. Check法人独立性: no disguised fund occupation across group entities.
