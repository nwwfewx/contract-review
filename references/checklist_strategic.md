# Strategic Review Checklist

## Reverse Thinking
1. Start from worst-case failure and backtrack clause weaknesses.
2. Test whether one weak clause can trigger chain failures.

## Negotiation Leverage
1. Identify non-linear value clauses (payment timing, control rights, termination triggers).
2. Separate must-have terms from tradable terms.

## Process Control
1. Convert uncertain obligations into measurable milestones.
2. Add evidence retention obligations for key performance and payment conditions.

## Counterparty Reliability
1. Evaluate履约 capacity, liquidity stress, and governance fragility.
2. Test exposure to related-party tunneling and fund occupation risk.

## Resilience
1. Ensure fallback paths for breach, delay, and document conflict.
2. Ensure key terms remain enforceable under adverse scenarios.
